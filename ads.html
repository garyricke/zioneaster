Newspaper Advertising

Placing the News-Miner Ad

The Fairbanks Daily News-Miner is the most effective paid print channel for reaching Fairbanks residents who are not already in your email or social networks — especially older adults and those less active online.

Call their advertising department to place a display ad. Ask specifically about their Church & Faith community section and their rates for the March 31 – April 2 editions (the days immediately before Good Friday). The print ad image (Ad 01) is ready to send as a PNG file attachment.

Tips for the Newsminer Call

  • Ask for a display ad, not a classified. Display ads include your image.
  • Request the church listings or community events section for lower rates.
  • Mention you have a print-ready image file — no design work needed on their end.
  • Ask about a 3-day run: Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday before Good Friday.
  • Typical size: 2-column × 4-inch display ad (confirm with their rep).

Other Free Channels Worth Using

  • Nextdoor Fairbanks & North Pole — post the square ad with a short message. Free and very effective locally.
  • UAF Campus bulletin boards — print the vertical flyer and post in the Wood Center and Rasmuson Library.
  • Zion's Facebook page — post the square ad, pin it to the top, share to your personal profile.
  • Local Facebook community groups — "Fairbanks Community Board," "North Pole Neighbors," etc.
